# Privacy Policy for Bracket Buddy

**Last Updated: February 13, 2026**

Bracket Buddy collects **no personal data**. The App operates entirely offline and does not:

- Collect or transmit personal information
- Send data to external servers or cloud services
- Use analytics or tracking
- Store user data outside the device
- Process payment information
- Access your contacts, location, or files

## Local Storage

All settings and preferences (bracket count, EV spacing, camera limits, 1/3-stop toggle) are stored **locally on your device only** using the device's built-in storage. This data is never transmitted to us or any third party.

## Camera Permissions

Bracket Buddy does not require or request access to your device's camera. The app is a calculation tool for exposure bracketing and does not capture, store, or process images.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, or services that collect user data.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the project repository or support channels.

## Compliance

This App complies with applicable privacy laws and regulations including:
- Google Play Store Developer Program Policies
- GDPR (where applicable)
- CCPA (where applicable)
